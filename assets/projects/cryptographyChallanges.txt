Crypto CTF Project

This project is a collection of custom cryptography-based Capture The
Flag (CTF) exercises designed to strengthen practical cybersecurity and
problem-solving skills. It focuses on applying cryptographic concepts
through interactive puzzles that require participants to analyze encoded
data, identify hidden patterns, and recover secret information.

The project incorporates techniques such as XOR operations, Base64
encoding, hexadecimal manipulation, classical ciphers, and multi-step
decoding processes. It encourages hands-on learning by combining logical
reasoning with scripting and cryptanalysis.

Developed as part of a cybersecurity portfolio, this repository
demonstrates an understanding of cryptographic principles, challenge
design, and secure coding practices while providing an engaging learning
experience for CTF enthusiasts and aspiring security professionals.
